Your safety and comfort are our priority. Report any ragging or teasing incidents confidentially.

Relevant Anti-Ragging Laws

  • Ragging is a criminal offence under Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1997.
  • Institutions must file FIRs for reported ragging incidents.
  • Students involved may face suspension or expulsion.
  • Strict disciplinary actions are taken under UGC guidelines.
