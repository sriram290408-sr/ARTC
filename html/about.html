Our Mission

The Anti-Ragging Committee (ARTC) works to prevent ragging and promote harmony in the institution. We ensure that every student feels safe, supported, and empowered to speak up against any form of harassment or bullying.

Confidential Reports

Your identity remains protected when you report any incident.

Student Support

We guide and assist students affected by any kind of ragging.

Awareness Programs

Workshops, events, and sessions to promote respect and unity.

Emergency Assistance

Immediate help for any student facing distress or unsafe situations.

Regular Monitoring

Periodic inspections and evaluations to maintain a safe environment.

Anonymous Feedback

Students can share their experiences and suggestions confidentially.
